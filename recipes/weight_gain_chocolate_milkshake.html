Weight Gain Chocolate Milkshake

Description

This is for all of you who need a high protein milkshake to gain weight and build muscle. It's in liquid form so you won't even know once you have finished it.

Preparation and Cooking Time

  • Preparation Time: 5 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 0 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 scoop (30 g) whey protein
  • 1 cup (240 ml) whole milk
  • 1 medium (125 g) frozen banana
  • 2 tbsp (32 g) peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp (10 g) cocoa powder
  • 6-8 ice cubes

How to make it?

  1. Place all ingredients into a high powered blender and blend until smooth. Adjust the ice as preferred to get a thicker or thinner shake.
